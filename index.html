FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us.

Is Prop Hunt free to use?
Yes! Prop Hunt is completely free for tenants to search for properties and contact landlords. For landlords, we offer a free tier to list a certain number of properties, with premium options available for those who need to list more.
How are landlords and listings verified?
We have a multi-step verification process for landlords, which may include identity and property ownership checks. This helps us maintain a high-quality, secure platform for everyone. Look for the "Verified" badge on listings.
Can I apply for a property directly through the app?
Absolutely. Our platform allows you to fill out a comprehensive rental application and submit it directly to landlords, making the process faster and more convenient.
What types of properties can be listed?
Landlords can list a wide variety of residential and land properties, including apartments, houses, condos, and townhouses. We are focused on connecting people with homes.
